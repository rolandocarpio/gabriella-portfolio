About Me

Over the past four years, I've worked on a variety of film productions, including feature-length films, music videos, short films, commercials, student films, as well as AFI, USC, and Columbia graduate thesis films. I've transitioned from being a Production Assistant to taking on various roles, such as Second Assistant Camera, Gaffer, Key Grip, Best Electric, Production Designer, Prop Master, and more.

All the while, I've dedicated myself to learning the technical skills and processes of production, simultaneously nurturing and expanding my profound interest in the theory and history of film and media. While at Columbia, I became the University News Photo Deputy at Columbia Daily Spectator, started freelance photography, attended numerous film screenings, and extensively networked with alumni, peers, and accomplished professionals in the industry.

My humanist media approach to photography and film constantly pushes me to continue evolving my storytelling capabilities and expanding my repertoire of technical skills. When I'm not behind the lens, you'll likely find me rewatching Spirited Away, embarking on a hike, immersed in video games, or baking cookies.
